# Troubleshooting Wizard for Calf Issues

## Diagnosis Trees

### 1. Scours
- **Symptoms:** Diarrhea, lethargy, loss of appetite.
  - **Step 1:** Check for dehydration. 
    - **If Yes:** Start hydration therapy. 
    - **If No:** Proceed to next symptom.
  - **Step 2:** Check dietary changes. 
    - **If Yes:** Revert to previous diet and monitor. 
    - **If No:** Check for infections or parasites. 

### 2. Poor Growth
- **Symptoms:** Weight loss, stunted growth, general weakness.
  - **Step 1:** Evaluate feeding practices. 
    - **If Inadequate:** Adjust feeding plans to ensure sufficient nutrition.
    - **If Adequate:** Check for underlying health issues.
  - **Step 2:** Check for parasites.
    - **If Present:** Administer appropriate deworming treatment.
    - **If Absent:** Investigate other health concerns.

### 3. Respiratory Issues
- **Symptoms:** Coughing, nasal discharge, labored breathing.
  - **Step 1:** Check for environmental factors. 
    - **If Poor Ventilation:** Improve barn air quality. 
    - **If Good Ventilation:** Look for signs of pneumonia or infections.
  - **Step 2:** Check for fever.
    - **If Fever:** Consult veterinarian for possible treatments.
    - **If No Fever:** Monitor and ensure adequate care.

---

This troubleshooting wizard is designed to assist with the quick identification of common calf issues and enhance recovery opportunities. Always consult with veterinary professionals for severe cases and persistent symptoms.
